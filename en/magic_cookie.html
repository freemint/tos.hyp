11.2 MAGX_COOKIE, status_bits

A bit-vector, which is defined as follows:

Bit-00 = MagiC task-manager active at present?
Bit-01 = Reserved
..  
Bit-31 = Reserved

Warning: The specified bits in this LONG value are read-only, i.e. the may not be changed.
